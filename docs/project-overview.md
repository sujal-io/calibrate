# Calibrate AI

## Tagline

Calibrate your resume. Bridge your skill gaps. Get interview-ready.

---

# Overview

Calibrate AI is an AI-powered resume gap analysis platform that helps students and early-career software engineers understand how well their resume matches a specific job description.

Instead of relying only on keyword matching, Calibrate performs semantic analysis using Large Language Models (LLMs) and embeddings to identify missing skills, highlight strengths, and generate actionable improvement suggestions.

The goal is to help users improve their resumes based on real job requirements before applying.

---

# Problem Statement

Most students manually compare their resumes with job descriptions, making it difficult to identify skill gaps and prioritize learning.

Existing ATS checkers mostly rely on keyword matching and provide limited actionable feedback.

Calibrate aims to provide intelligent semantic analysis and personalized recommendations using Generative AI.
